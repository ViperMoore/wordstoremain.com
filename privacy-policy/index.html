Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 June 2026  ·  Words to Remain is operated by Scrypto Computers EOOD, registered in Bulgaria (EU).

We keep this simple: we only collect information you give us directly, we use it only to deliver your speech, and we never sell it to anyone.

1. Who we are

Words to Remain is a personalised eulogy writing service operated by Scrypto Computers EOOD, a company registered in Bulgaria, European Union. Our trading name is Words to Remain and our contact email is hello@wordstoremain.com.

For the purposes of EU GDPR, Scrypto Computers EOOD is the data controller for personal information collected through this website.

2. What information we collect

We collect personal information only when you provide it to us directly. This happens in two ways:

  • Order form (via Tally): When you place an order, we collect your name, email address, and the personal details you share about yourself and your loved one — including their name, age, memories, and any other information you choose to provide to help us write your speech.
  • Contact form (via Formspree): When you send us a message, we collect your name, email address, and the content of your message.

We do not use cookies, tracking scripts, or analytics tools on this website. We do not collect any information passively.

3. How we use your information

We use the information you provide solely for the following purposes:

  • To write and deliver your personalised eulogy speech
  • To communicate with you about your order, including revisions
  • To respond to your enquiries and messages
  • To process your payment securely through Stripe (see Section 5)

We do not use your information for marketing unless you explicitly ask us to keep you updated.

4. Sensitive personal information

The information you share about your loved one — their life, your memories, and the circumstances of their passing — is treated with the utmost care and confidentiality. It is used only to write your speech and is never shared with any third party for any reason.

5. Third-party services

We use a small number of trusted third-party services to operate this website:

  • Tally (tally.so): Our order form provider. Tally processes and stores your form submissions on our behalf. Tally Privacy Policy →
  • Formspree (formspree.io): Our contact form provider. Messages submitted via our contact form are processed by Formspree. Formspree Privacy Policy →
  • Stripe: Our payment processor. When you make a payment, your payment details are handled directly and securely by Stripe. We never see or store your card details. Stripe Privacy Policy →
  • Gmail / Google: We use Gmail to deliver your completed speech to your inbox. Your email address is used for this purpose only.

All third-party providers are required to handle your data in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

6. How long we keep your information

We retain your order information — including the details you shared about your loved one and the speech we wrote — for up to 12 months, in case you need to request revisions or contact us about your order. After this period, your data is deleted.

Contact form enquiries are retained for up to 6 months.

7. Your rights under GDPR

As a resident of the European Union or United Kingdom, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You can ask us to correct inaccurate information.
  • Right to erasure: You can ask us to delete your personal data, subject to any legal obligations we may have.
  • Right to restrict processing: You can ask us to limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability: You can request your data in a portable format.
  • Right to object: You can object to our processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at hello@wordstoremain.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data transfers

Some of our third-party providers may process data outside the European Union. Where this occurs, we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place in accordance with GDPR requirements, such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

9. Children's privacy

Our service is intended for adults only. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

11. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

  • Email: hello@wordstoremain.com
  • Via our contact form

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. In Bulgaria, this is the Commission for Personal Data Protection (CPDP).
