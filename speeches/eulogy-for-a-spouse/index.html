Frequently Asked Questions

Good to know

How long does it take to receive a eulogy for a spouse or partner?

Standard orders are delivered by email within 24 hours. Priority orders are delivered within 1 hour and include WhatsApp support if you need to reach us quickly.

Do I need to write anything myself?

No writing is required. You complete a short questionnaire sharing memories, stories, and details about your spouse or partner, and we write the full eulogy for you from that material.

Can I request changes if it's not quite right?

Yes. Standard orders include 1 round of revisions, and Priority orders include 3 rounds, so you can adjust tone, length, or specific details until it feels right.

Is the eulogy really personalised, or a generic template?

It's written entirely from the memories and details you provide about your spouse or partner — never a generic template with names swapped in. No two eulogies we write are the same.
